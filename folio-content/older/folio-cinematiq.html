Description
The independent film festival industry is a business sector that can greatly benefit from improved UX for technology automation. Cinematiq hired me to help flesh out core interactions and develop a style-guide for their web-based automation tools. This was a fun and fairly quick project which involved stakeholder interviews, wireframes, basic visual design, and basic front-end-engineering.
