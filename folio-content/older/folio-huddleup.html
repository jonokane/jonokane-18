Description
HuddleUp is the result of great ideas from the minds of the Net Promoter Score System, with the goal of helping teams improve their workflow and collaboration. Tangible UX was hired to evolve their prototype into a powerful, usable, responsive app.
link https://www.huddleuphub.com/
  • Sketches of early ideation, organization, and concepts for the HuddleUp app. Showing the working process of the FTUX, as well as the "enticement" component of HuddleUp. The challenge was to get users into the product, while collecting the appropriate sign-up info, and then answering the question, "how do we bring people back to the app?".
  • The sketches transformed to wireframe for the logged-in HuddleUp homepage.
  • The final product, with visual design by Heather Shaw-Willis.
